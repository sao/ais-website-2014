We are Advanced

Advanced Industrial Services is a well established organization providing the highest quality services offered to respective clients. Certified as a woman owned business, AIS was incorporated in 1997 and has been the leader in its field ever since. We have built a well known reputation because of our constant push for safety, cost effective solutions and customer satisfaction. AIS offers a variety of services from tank and vessel cleaning to high pressure water blasting. Each task is done with caution and care making safety our number one valued goal. Our highly trained on-site supervisors are experienced to handle any job safely and efficiently. With over 20 years of experience, AIS knows the importance of doing every job in a professional manner. We are available any time to ensure we meet the needs and requirements for both small and large producers with the intention to establish strategic long term alliance relationships with all of our clients. The oil and gas industry has experienced numerous changes over the last decade. With changes it demands organizations such as Advanced Industrial Services to embrace the challenges of the 21th century. To withhold our reputation as the leaders in our field, we take great pride in developing a new generation of highly skilled and motivated work force. Doing so creates the best intent for a safe working environment and provides the highest quality and cost effective field services.
